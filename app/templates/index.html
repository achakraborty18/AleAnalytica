AB InBev Clustering

Dendrogram

Hierarchical Clustering Technique

  • Employed Ward's linkage method to perform hierarchical clustering.
  • Calculate the linkage matrix based on distances between data points.

Cluster Creation and Feature Selection

  • Determined clusters using a distance threshold of 140, based on the generated linkage matrix.
  • Calculated the top 50 most important features in each cluster using Random Forest Regressor

The 8 clusters

Harmony Quaffs - The Refined Sippers

HarmonyQuaffs, unveils The Refined Sippers—a group seamlessly merging modern connectivity with timeless values. With a preference for moderate internet use, they embody authenticity, freedom, stable relationships, and self-reliance. Socially active and retired, they balance online presence with leisure activities, appreciating quality spirits without being overly price-conscious.

Quality Connoisseurs - Discerning Shoppers

Quality Connoisseurs or SophisticateSelects, represents discerning shoppers who highly value quality and authenticity. With elevated internet use and education levels, they trust established brands, explore diverse beverages, and prioritize savoring full-flavored beers for a well-deserved reward. Tech-savvy and engaged in home-based entertainment, this cluster is receptive to marketing emphasizing product quality and brand authenticity on digital platforms.

Relaxed Connectors - Casual Browsers

Relaxed Connectors, epitomizes a group with a penchant for leisure and ease of access, embodying the persona of Alex, the Casual Browser. Typically aged between 25-40 and often freelancers, their interests in social media, casual gaming, and yoga define a laid-back disposition. Focused on relaxation and convenience, engaging this cluster requires online-centric marketing with a relaxed tone and convenience-focused messaging.

Social Sippers - Dynamic Aficionados

Dynamic Aficionados, embodies a lively and socially engaged cohort with a distinctive essence. This group passionately explores diverse beer types and brands, embracing a well-rounded lifestyle rich in diverse entertainment. Trust and reliability are foundational to their shopping preferences, reflecting a desire for authentic experiences. Dynamic Aficionados exemplify a harmonious fusion of social connections, beer appreciation, and a vibrant approach to life.

Serenity Sippers - The Thoughtful Homebody

"Serenity Sippers," represents a diverse age group with a lifestyle blending modernity and serene, home-based experiences in beer enjoyment. Emphasizing authenticity and meaningful relationships, this cluster finds a balanced approach to digital engagement, preferring tranquil moments of beer enjoyment at home.

Ready-to-Drink Relaxers - Effortless Indulgers

"Effortless Indulgers," encompasses individuals aged 40-55 with a penchant for a laid-back lifestyle. This cohort, often found in roles like sales managers, expresses a fondness for sports, barbecues, and casual gatherings, emphasizing convenience and easy-to-consume products.

Adventurous Qualitarians - Heritage Voyagers

"Adventurous Qualitarians," comprises individuals aged 30-45 with an adventurous spirit. This cohort, often associated with travel blogging, expresses a keen interest in adventure travel, cultural experiences, and outdoor activities.

Beer Connoisseurs - ChillBrew Crew

"ChillBrew Crew," is composed of individuals aged 35-50 who prioritize beer as their regular drink. With a commitment to enduring relationships and self-reliance, this cluster mirrors a preference for a simpler and more comforting lifestyle.
